Rules

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • In each turn, a player rolls two dices as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score
  • BUT, if the player rolls two 1s, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • AND, if the player rolls two 6s, all his SCORE gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • The first player to reach Winning Score (Default 100) on GLOBAL score wins the game!
